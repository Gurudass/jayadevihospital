FAQ

Explore our Frequently Asked Questions regarding healthcare services.

Question: Does the Jayadevi Bailey Hospital have ambulance services ?

Yes, our multispecialty hospital is equipped with ambulance facilities to ensure swift and efficient transportation for emergency cases and patient transfers.

Question: What specialties does the hospital cover ?

Our hospital covers a wide range of specialties, including but not limited to cardiology, orthopedics, gynecology, gastroenterology, and neurology.

How are medical emergencies handled ?

Our hospital has a dedicated emergency department with trained staff available 24/7 to handle medical emergencies promptly. The ambulance service further enhances our ability to respond quickly.

Question: Are there specialized pediatric services ?

Yes, our hospital provides specialized pediatric care with experienced pediatricians and a child-friendly environment to address the unique healthcare needs of children.

Can patients transfer seamlessly between departments ?

Absolutely, our multispecialty hospital ensures seamless patient transfers between departments, promoting coordinated care and comprehensive treatment plans. The ambulance service facilitates smooth transitions when needed.
